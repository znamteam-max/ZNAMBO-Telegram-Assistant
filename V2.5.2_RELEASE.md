# ZNAMBO V2.5.2

- Every rendered task, history item, reminder policy, and campaign can open an editable entity card.
- Russian weekday appointments are normalized deterministically; `во вторник к 10.20` resolves to the next future Tuesday at 10:20.
- Importance now keeps base importance, temporary urgency, and `importanceMode` separate and uses human labels.
- Duplicate requests always answer and open the existing item instead of silently doing nothing.
- Future campaign events require clarification before completion.
- `/admin_repair_v252 preview|apply` repairs known production data issues and stores an audit snapshot for rollback.
