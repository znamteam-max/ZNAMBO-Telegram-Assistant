# V2.8.0 - Reminder Policy UX, Snooze and Plan Routing

Release date: 2026-06-13

## Implemented

- `/plan` and `/dashboard` render the live Plan directly; slash commands clear pending item and
  reminder-policy edit sessions.
- Plan renders active reminder policies inline under their owning tasks. Persistent until-done
  reminders use the visible `❗` marker; unattached policies appear only as cleanup state.
- User-facing Plan, task, reminder, edit and conflict dates include Russian weekday labels.
- Reminder-policy edit sessions bind cadence-only replies to the selected item and prevent creation
  of garbage tasks such as `Каждый час с 8 утра до 18`.
- High-frequency policies support snooze for 30 minutes, 1 hour, 2 hours or until tomorrow. Policy
  and item snooze is enforced by claim SQL, the reconciler, materialization and a final pre-delivery
  check in the runner.
- Normal reminders expose Done, one-hour snooze and item editing controls.
- Complex three-reminder input produces a confirmation preview with three reminder intents and
  targeted active-window/monthly clarifications.
- `/admin_repair_v280 preview|apply` conservatively repairs the known cadence-only garbage task
  only when the target is unambiguous, leaves Yandex Calendar untouched and records audit entries.

During rollout, the snooze-aware claim query was changed from an outer join lock to `NOT EXISTS`
after production PostgreSQL rejected locking the nullable join side. The complex-request
normalizer was also extended for plural `показания счётчиков`.

## Remaining Limitations

- Monthly day-range policies are represented in the complex-request preview and require explicit
  confirmation before commit.
- Owner Telegram acceptance remains for the visual `/plan` and `/dashboard` flow, reminder-edit
  context, and interactive snooze buttons.
