## Implemented

- Weekly recurring reminders with missing time remain typed drafts until explicit time selection.
- Smart commit and policy execution now guard against canonical recurring rules without time.
- Reminder setup parses Russian natural windows such as `с 8 утра до 8 вечера`, `с восьми утра до восьми вечера`, `с 10 утра до 6 вечера`, and `до конца дня`.
- Cadence edits on recurring items update the recurring policy instead of creating one-day local windows.
- Reminder runner supports recurring interval windows, including weekly hourly 08:00-20:00 until done.
- Monthly 15-19 day-range reminders draft cleanly without time and use `monthly_days:15,16,17,18,19@HH:mm` with explicit time.
- Plan/reminder rendering removes duplicate `❗`, omits `без времени` for unscheduled recurring rows, and formats 300-minute intervals as 5-hour reminders.
- Item card marker controls added: Auto / Show / Hide via `persistentMarkerMode` metadata.
- Filler titles normalize, for example `О том, чтобы я решил вопрос...` -> `Решить вопрос...`.
- `/admin_repair_v2120 preview|apply` repairs mirror duplicate policies and moves the broken Fedotov policy to review/paused without Yandex Calendar changes.
